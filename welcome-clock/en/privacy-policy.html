Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Welcome Clock ("the App") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

Photos

The App accesses your device's photo library solely to display photos in the slideshow feature. Photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted to external servers.

Local Storage

The App stores the following preferences locally on your device only. This data is never sent to any server.

  • Clock style (font, size, color, position)
  • Slideshow settings (interval, etc.)
  • Language preference
  • Ad-free status

Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal Information — We do not collect name, email address, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Location Data — We do not access GPS or location services.
  • Device Identifiers — We do not collect device IDs.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:

Service Data Collected Purpose
Google AdMob Device info, advertising ID, ad interaction data Ad serving (settings screen only)

The clock screen does not display any advertisements. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional purchase to remove advertisements, processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. The App does not collect or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored on your device is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through the App or on this page.
