# Welcome Clock - Support

Last updated: February 28, 2026

## About the App

Welcome Clock is a clock and photo slideshow application. Display the current time beautifully over your favorite photos from your device's photo library.

### Key Features

- **Clock Display**: Choose from multiple font styles (Roboto Mono, Orbitron, Share Tech Mono, VT323) and customize colors, size, and position
- **Photo Slideshow**: Automatically cycle through photos from your device's library at your preferred interval
- **Customization**: Adjust clock style, font size, colors, display position, and slideshow settings
- **Ad-Free Option**: Remove ads with an optional in-app purchase

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I select photos for the slideshow?

Open the Settings screen and tap the photo selection option. You can choose photos from your device's photo library. All photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

### How do I change the clock style?

Open the Settings screen to customize the clock font, size, color, and position on the screen.

### How do I remove ads?

You can remove ads by purchasing the Pro version through the in-app purchase option in the Settings screen. This is a one-time purchase.

### I purchased Pro but ads are still showing

Try the following steps:

1. Close and reopen the app
2. Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases"
3. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID / Google account used for the purchase
4. If the issue persists, please contact us at the email below

### The app is not displaying my photos

1. Make sure you have granted the app permission to access your photo library
2. Go to your device's Settings > Privacy > Photos and ensure Welcome Clock has access
3. Try restarting the app

### The clock is not visible or hard to read

Try adjusting the clock color or position in the Settings screen to improve visibility against your photo backgrounds.
