# Privacy Policy - Welcome Clock

Last updated: February 5, 2026

## Overview

Welcome Clock ("the App") is a clock and photo slideshow application. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information Collection and Use

### Photos

The App accesses your device's photo library solely to display photos in the slideshow feature. **Photos are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted to external servers.**

### App Settings

The App stores your preferences (clock style, font size, colors, display position, slideshow interval, etc.) locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data is never sent to any server.

### Personal Information

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

- Name or email address
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or telemetry

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

The free version of the App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob on the settings screen only. The clock screen does not display any advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's privacy policy. This may include:

- Device information (device model, OS version)
- Advertising identifiers
- IP address
- Ad interaction data

For more information, please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional donation to remove advertisements, processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by the respective platform. The App does not collect or store any payment information.

## Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. The App does not use any remote servers or cloud storage.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
